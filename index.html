Welcome to
SECiD

The site to connect with UNAM's Data Science Alumni Society.

SECiD (Sociedad de Egresados en Ciencia de Datos, A. C.) is a vibrant professional network empowering UNAM data science graduates. Our goal is to foster meaningful connections, drive professional growth, and create opportunities for our community through networking events, exclusive job postings, continuous learning initiatives, and collaborations that support and boost the ongoing success of data science professionals from the National Autonomous University of Mexico (UNAM).

Our Mission

Unite UNAM Data Science graduates

Created to gather the Data Science community from graduates of academic programs at UNAM.

Innovate within the community but also with the public and private sectors

We understand the deep impact data science and machine learning can bring to society. We seek to collaborate in interdisciplinary settings that impact all parts of society.

Promote technology and research projects

Use our community knowledge to boost projects.

Democratize technological knowledge

Bring our expertise to all parts of society.
